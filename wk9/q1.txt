1. Setting up a network - e.g. connecting all the houses in a suburb to the NBN using as little wiring as possible
  -Vertices: houses
  -Edges   : network cables between houses
  -Weights : length of cables

2. Clustering items in a dataset - e.g. social networks, online shopping recommendation systems
  -Vertices: items to be categorised
  -Edges   : relationship between two items
  -Weights : level of similarity/difference between two items
